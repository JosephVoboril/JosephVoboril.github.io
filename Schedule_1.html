What Are Schedule 1 Drugs?

Schedule 1 drugs are drugs that are completely banned and under no circumstance are allowed to be used. If you are caught with schedule 1 drugs the punishment you receive will vary. If you were selling the drugs vs taking the drugs the punishemnt for the seller would be worse then the punishment for the buyer.
