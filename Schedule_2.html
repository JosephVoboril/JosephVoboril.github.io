What are Schedule 2 drugs?

Schedule 2 drugs are drugs that once again are completely banned however the main difference is that very few doctors can prescribe Schedule 2 drugs. Drugs under schedule 2 can be complicated when being prescribed, as the conditions for getting a prescribtion for a substance under schedule 2 not only need to be very specific but also need to be received from a very specific doctor. Your doctor will most likely be able to prescribe schedule 2 drugs but they do need the right licenses

  • Methamphetamine
  • Fentanyl
  • Opium
